想要在雅思口语和写作中达到 **Band 8+**，关键不在于使用“生僻词”（Obscure words），而在于使用**“精准词” (Precise Vocabulary)** 和 **“不常见但地道的搭配” (Less Common Lexical Items)**。

如果你的词汇库里全是 *good, bad, many, important*，考官会认为你的语言能力停留在 B2 (Band 6)。要达到 C1/C2 (Band 8+)，你需要将这些“通用词”替换为具有**特定语境、情感色彩和专业度**的词汇。

以下是我为你精心整理的 **100 组 8 分+ 替换词库**，分为 10 个核心维度。

### 💡 如何使用这份清单（避坑指南）

1.  **不要“过度包装” (Over-sophistication)：**
    如果你在口语中每一句话都用 *plethora* 或 *erudite*，考官会觉得你在背单词书，这反而会降低你的 **Naturalness (自然度)** 分数。
    *   **口语：** 选择 7-8 分的词（如 *substantial, pivotal, breathtaking*）。
    *   **写作：** 可以大胆使用 8-9 分的词（如 *deleterious, accentuate, paradoxically*）。

2.  **关注“搭配” (Collocations)：**
    单词不能单独记。
    *   错误：$\text{Big gap} \rightarrow \text{Colossal gap}$ (虽通，但不够地道)
    *   正确：$\text{Big gap} \rightarrow \text{Stark disparity}$ (极大的差距 —— 这是一个 8 分+ 的地道搭配)

3.  **练习方法：**
    每天选 5 组词，尝试用它们写 3 个关于雅思真题的句子。例如，选 `detrimental`，写一句关于 `Social Media` 对 `Mental Health` 的影响。


这道题的 Part 3 问题可能是：

**"Should prisoners be taught personal and job skills while in prison?"**

---

## 示范回答

"Absolutely, and I think this is actually one of the more **overlooked** solutions to reducing crime. Personal skills — things like communication, emotional control, and taking responsibility — are honestly what many offenders lacked in the first place. Without those, even the best job training won't stick.

And then on top of that, specific job skills give them something **concrete** to walk out with. Whether it's construction, coding, or cooking — it doesn't matter. What matters is that they have a real reason to stay on the right path, because now they have something to lose.

What I find most compelling is this: **keeping someone in prison costs society far more than training them**. So from a purely practical standpoint, skill-based rehabilitation isn't just kind — it's smart. It reduces reoffending, cuts government spending, and honestly, it gives people a second shot at a decent life. I think that's something most people can get behind."

## 亮点在哪里

| 技巧         | 体现                                                             |

| 先给立场，再层层展开 | Absolutely → personal → job → big picture                      |
| 转折过渡自然     | "And then on top of that…" / "What I find most compelling is…" |
| 有数据/逻辑感    | "costs society far more than training them"                    |
