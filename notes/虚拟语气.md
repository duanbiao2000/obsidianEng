**底层需求：理解虚拟语气三大类的核心逻辑，而非死记硬背公式。**

---

## 本质：虚拟语气 = 时态后移一格

越"不现实"，时态退得越远。

---

## 一、If 型（条件虚拟）

**与现实相反的假设**

|类型|从句|主句|
|---|---|---|
|与现在相反|if + did|would do|
|与过去相反|if + had done|would have done|
|混合|if + had done|would do|

> If I **were** rich, I **would travel** the world.（现在不富） If I **had studied** harder, I **would have passed**.（过去没努力） If I **had saved** money, I **would be** free now.（混合）

---

## 二、Wish 型（愿望虚拟）

**对现实的遗憾/不满**

|对象|结构|例句|
|---|---|---|
|现在|wish + did/were|I wish I **knew** the answer.|
|过去|wish + had done|I wish I **had come** earlier.|
|将来（抱怨）|wish + would do|I wish it **would stop** raining.|

> wish 后时态同样后移一格。 will → would 表示对他人行为的不满/期望。

---

## 三、Should 型（虚拟命令）

**建议、要求、命令类动词后，that从句用 should do 或直接用原形**

触发词：==suggest / recommend / insist / demand / require / order / propose==

> I suggest that he **(should) leave** immediately. It is important that she **(should) be** informed.

> 美式英语倾向直接用原形（虚拟式），英式保留 should。

---

## 一张图看逻辑

```
现实越远 → 时态退越多

Should型：原形（最轻，只是"应该"）
Wish/If现在：过去式
Wish/If过去：过去完成式
```

---

## If 型 — 10句

**与现在相反**

1. If I **were** a bird, I **would fly** away.
2. If she **knew** his number, she **would call** him.
3. If we **had** more time, we **would finish** it today.
4. If he **weren't** so stubborn, things **would be** easier.
5. If they **lived** closer, we **would meet** more often.

**与过去相反**

6. If I **had left** earlier, I **would have caught** the train.
7. If you **had told** me, I **would have helped** you.
8. If she **had applied**, she **would have gotten** the job.
9. If we **hadn't argued**, he **would have stayed**.
10. If they **had invested** in 2010, they **would have made** a fortune.

---

## Wish 型 — 10句

**对现在的遗憾**

1. I wish I **were** taller.
2. I wish I **knew** how to cook.
3. She wishes she **lived** in Tokyo.
4. He wishes he **could** drive.
5. I wish the meeting **weren't** so long.

**对过去的遗憾**

6. I wish I **had studied** medicine.
7. She wishes she **hadn't said** that.
8. I wish we **had met** sooner.

**对他人行为的不满（将来/现在）** 9. I wish you **would stop** interrupting me. 10. I wish the government **would do** something about it.

---

## Should 型 — 10句

**建议/要求类动词 + that + 原形**

1. I suggest that he **leave** now.
2. She recommended that we **hire** a lawyer.
3. The manager insisted that everyone **be** on time.
4. They demanded that the report **be** submitted by Friday.
5. The policy requires that all staff **complete** training.
6. It is vital that she **attend** the hearing.
7. It is crucial that the data **be** protected.
8. It is important that you **not miss** this deadline.
9. The court ordered that he **pay** compensation.
10. The committee proposed that the rule **be** abolished.

---

**记忆锚点复习：**

| 类型     | 现在错位             | 过去错位     |
| ------ | ---------------- | -------- |
| If     | did/were         | had done |
| Wish   | did/were / could | had done |
| Should | 原形（无错位）          | —        |
这是同一个现象——**T 浊化（T-voicing / flapping）**，就是之前说的闪音化。

`bottom` 中 `-tt-` 处于重读元音后 + 非重读元音前 → `/t/` → `/ɾ/`，听起来像 `boddem`。

## 不发生浊化的情况

|条件|例子|原因|
|---|---|---|
|T 在重读音节前|**t**omato, re**t**urn|不满足"重读后"条件|
|T 在词尾|ca**t**, bi**t**|后面没有元音|
|T 后接辅音|ac**t**ive, op**t**ion|后面不是元音|
|英式英语|water, better|英式不做 flapping|

---

**一句话记住规律：** 重读元音 → **t** → 非重读元音，美式必闪音。位置对了就变，位置不对就不变。
