**本质：ESL学习者用语法正确的句子，但缺少英语的"缓冲层"，听起来像在下命令或质问。**

---

**1 — 请求类（最高频问题）**

|ESL说|听起来|Native会说|
|---|---|---|
|_"Give me the menu."_|命令|_"Could I grab a menu?"_|
|_"I want coffee."_|冷淡|_"I'll have a coffee, please."_|
|_"Tell me the answer."_|强硬|_"Do you happen to know the answer?"_|
|_"Explain this to me."_|质问|_"Could you walk me through this?"_|

---

**2 — 问题类**

|ESL说|问题所在|Native会说|
|---|---|---|
|_"Why did you do that?"_|像审问|_"What made you decide to do that?"_|
|_"How old are you?"_|太直接|_"If you don't mind me asking, how old are you?"_|
|_"What is your salary?"_|失礼|通常不问，或*"Is the pay pretty good there?"*|
|_"Are you married?"_|突兀|自然话题引出，不直接问|

---

**3 — 否定/拒绝类**

|ESL说|听起来|Native会说|
|---|---|---|
|_"No, that's wrong."_|攻击性|_"Hmm, I'm not sure that's quite right."_|
|_"I don't like it."_|生硬|_"It's not really my thing."_|
|_"I can't do this."_|死板|_"I'm not sure I'm the best person for this."_|
|_"That's a bad idea."_|冒犯|_"I wonder if there might be another way?"_|

---

**4 — 意见类**

|ESL说|问题|Native会说|
|---|---|---|
|_"You are wrong."_|直接对立|_"I see it a bit differently, actually."_|
|_"This is boring."_|无缓冲|_"It's not really holding my attention."_|
|_"Your idea is not good."_|冒犯|_"I'm not totally convinced, to be honest."_|

---

**5 — 日常互动类**

|ESL说|感觉|Native会说|
|---|---|---|
|_"What do you want?"_|不耐烦|_"What can I do for you?"_|
|_"Wait."_|命令|_"Hold on a sec."_|
|_"I don't understand."_|平淡|_"Sorry, could you say that again?"_|
|_"You must do this."_|强迫|_"It'd probably be worth doing this."_|

---

**核心规律：**

英语礼貌的本质不是词汇，是**模糊化 + 降低确定性**

```
直接陈述  →  加情态动词    (must → might/could)
命令句   →  疑问句        (Do this → Could you...?)
负面评价  →  双重否定/迂回  (bad → not quite right)
确定语气  →  不确定语气    (is → seems to be)
```

> 听起来blunt的根本原因：**中文可以直接说事，英语需要先铺一层"我不确定/我只是建议"的外衣。**
