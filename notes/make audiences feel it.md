## 1. 形容体验和真实感

- make audiences feel it  
    “不仅让观众看到，还要让他们**真切感受到**（速度等）”。[[youtube](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=nrjH7qHe8WM)]​
    
- push it even further  
    在已经很厉害的基础上，“把某事推进到更高水平/做得更极致”。[[youtube](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=nrjH7qHe8WM)]​
    
- as authentic a racing movie as ever been  
    “史上尽可能**真实**的赛车电影”。as authentic a … as ever been = 极其真实。[[youtube](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=nrjH7qHe8WM)]​
    
- grounded quality  
    “很接地气、很真实、不浮夸的质感”。在影视里常指“真实可信的感觉”。[[youtube](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=nrjH7qHe8WM)]​
    
- we've accomplished something that hasn't been done before  
    “我们完成了前所未有的事”。很常见的宣传口径。[[youtube](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=nrjH7qHe8WM)]​
    
- pinnacle of motorsports  
    pinnacle = “顶点”；the pinnacle of X = “X 的巅峰”。这里是“赛车运动的巅峰”。[[youtube](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=nrjH7qHe8WM)]​
    
- the most authentic racing movie that's ever been made  
    最真实的赛车电影，用来强调“从未有过的真实感”。[[youtube](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=nrjH7qHe8WM)]​

## 2. 职业 / 梦想 / 野心相关表达

- what a thrill for us to be here  
    “能来这儿真是太刺激/太荣幸了”。thrill = 很强的兴奋感。[[youtube](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=nrjH7qHe8WM)]​
    
- dreamed of making (this project) my whole life  
    “一辈子的梦想项目”。[[youtube](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=nrjH7qHe8WM)]​
    
- keep us honest  
    字面是“让我们保持诚实”，真实语境里是“监督我们别太夸张/别太好莱坞”。[[youtube](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=nrjH7qHe8WM)]​
    
- if this is the last one I get to do, I'm going to be happy  
    “就算这是我最后一部片子，我也满足了”。常用于强调“这件事很有意义”。[[youtube](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=nrjH7qHe8WM)]​
    
- ambition is trying to challenge yourself or do something that hasn't been done before  
    其中的搭配：challenge yourself（挑战自己）、hasn't been done before（前所未有）。[[youtube](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=nrjH7qHe8WM)]​

## 3. F1 / 拍摄合作场景里的口语短语

- unprecedented access to the tracks  
    unprecedented access = “前所未有的准入权限/通行权限”。[[youtube](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=nrjH7qHe8WM)]​
    
- we might have to just move in. This is better than my garage.  
    开玩笑说“这里太棒了，我要搬来住了，比我车库还好”。move in = 搬进来住。[[youtube](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=nrjH7qHe8WM)]​
    
- all you guys have to do is be yourselves  
    “你们只要做自己就好”。拍摄时安抚对方常用句。[[youtube](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=nrjH7qHe8WM)]​
    
- treat them like any other driver  
    “把他们当普通车手对待”。like any other X = 和其他…没区别。[[youtube](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=nrjH7qHe8WM)]​
    
- we're using some Hollywood tricks  
    “我们会用一些好莱坞的小技巧（特效、拍摄手法等）”。[[youtube](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=nrjH7qHe8WM)]​
    
- it's just a real pleasure to be here  
    “在这儿真的很荣幸/很开心”。非常常见的正式口语套话。[[youtube](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=nrjH7qHe8WM)]​

## 4. 自嘲 / 幽默表达

- if we're in the way, tell us to f*** off. We f*** off very well.  
    in the way = “挡路，妨碍你们”。整体意思：“如果我们碍事，就直接叫我们滚，我们很会滚。” 很口语化的自嘲幽默。[[youtube](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=nrjH7qHe8WM)]​
    
- I go flat through Abbey and Copse  
    go flat = 不松油门/全油门通过弯道。这里是“我在这些弯都是全油门（装个高手）”。[[youtube](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=nrjH7qHe8WM)]​
    
- this is the man who's going to prove that I really was behind  
    behind 在这里有“落后”的意思；整体带点玩笑：“他会证明我真的亲自开过。”[[youtube](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=nrjH7qHe8WM)]​
    
- you have to ask some of the professionals if they'll call it driving  
    自嘲“是不是能算开车，还得问专业车手才知道”。if they'll call it X = 是否配得上叫做 X。[[youtube](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=nrjH7qHe8WM)]​
    
- I'm bragging now. I'm going to brag.  
    “我要开始吹牛了”。brag = 吹牛、得意地炫耀。[[youtube](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=nrjH7qHe8WM)]​
    
- they had to stop me. I kept smiling in the takes.  
    take = 一条镜头；keep doing = 不停地做。“我老是在镜头里笑，他们只好喊停”。[[youtube](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=nrjH7qHe8WM)]​
    
- I believe I'm the fastest driver of all time now.  
    很夸张的调侃：“我相信我是史上最快车手了。”[[youtube](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=nrjH7qHe8WM)]​

## 5. 训练 / 技术 / 操作相关

- my expectations were exceeded  
    “远超我的预期”。很常见正式表达。[[youtube](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=nrjH7qHe8WM)]​
    
- a roller coaster (feeling)  
    把体验比喻成“过山车”，常用来形容又刺激又起伏的体验。[[youtube](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=nrjH7qHe8WM)]​
    
- downforce / G-force  
    专业词：下压力 / G 力（重力加速度带来的压力）。[[youtube](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=nrjH7qHe8WM)]​
    
- it sucks the air out of your lungs  
    比喻：强大的 G 力 /速度“把肺里的气都挤出来了”，形容很夸张的体感。[[youtube](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=nrjH7qHe8WM)]​
    
- I go to sleep and see the track in my dreams  
    夸张说法：连睡觉做梦都在跑赛道，说明训练投入。[[youtube](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=nrjH7qHe8WM)]​
    
- been incredibly instrumental  
    be instrumental (in) = “起关键作用，非常重要”。[[youtube](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=nrjH7qHe8WM)]​

## 6. 赛车 / 电影行业里的常用搭配

- endurance racing / endurance driver  
    endurance = 耐力。endurance racing = 耐力赛（比如 24 小时）；endurance driver = 耐力赛车手。[[youtube](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=nrjH7qHe8WM)]​
    
- stadium-like atmosphere  
    “像体育场一样的氛围（观众多、气氛热烈）”。X-like atmosphere = 像 X 一样的氛围。[[youtube](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=nrjH7qHe8WM)]​
    
- part of Porsche's DNA  
    “深植在保时捷 DNA 里的东西” = 某品牌非常核心的特质。X is part of Y's DNA 很常见。[[youtube](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=nrjH7qHe8WM)]​
    
- provided the training / provide support  
    provide training/support = 提供训练 / 提供支持，是非常常用的工作场景搭配。[[youtube](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=nrjH7qHe8WM)]​
    
- to add authenticity  
    “增加真实感”。add authenticity to… = 让…更真实。[[youtube](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=nrjH7qHe8WM)]​
    
- shut down the Porsche Experience Center for us  
    shut down (场地/道路) for us = 为我们封场，用于拍摄或活动。[[youtube](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=nrjH7qHe8WM)]​
    
- had a wonderful relationship with them  
    “合作关系很好”。have a (great/wonderful) relationship with… 是商务常用说法。[[youtube](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=nrjH7qHe8WM)]​

## 7. 拍摄技术和设备表达

- bespoke lightweight camera packages  
    bespoke = 定制的；lightweight = 轻量；camera package = 摄影机整体方案/组件。[[youtube](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=nrjH7qHe8WM)]​
    
- sensor on a stick  
    开玩笑式说法：希望相机“就像一根棍子上的传感器那么小”。[[youtube](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=nrjH7qHe8WM)]​
    
- full-frame sensor, 4K  
    摄影常用：全画幅传感器、4K 分辨率。[[youtube](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=nrjH7qHe8WM)]​
    
- shallower depth of field  
    更浅的景深（背景更虚）。depth of field = 景深。[[youtube](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=nrjH7qHe8WM)]​
    
- vibration isolation  
    减振/隔振装置。[[youtube](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=nrjH7qHe8WM)]​
    
- variables  
    “变量、不确定因素”，如轮胎、路面、时间等。[[youtube](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=nrjH7qHe8WM)]​
    
- hide all the equipment  
    “把设备都藏起来”，在车里找空间塞录像设备。[[youtube](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=nrjH7qHe8WM)]​
    
- camera angles and points of view you've never seen before  
    “前所未见的机位和视角”。[[youtube](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=nrjH7qHe8WM)]​

## 8. 品牌合作 / 商务表达

- working hand in hand with their designers and engineers  
    hand in hand = 密切协作。[[youtube](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=nrjH7qHe8WM)]​
    
- tie in with the film  
    商业搭配：a product ties in with a film = 为电影推出联名/特别版产品。[[youtube](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=nrjH7qHe8WM)]​
    
- special edition  
    特别版、限量版。[[youtube](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=nrjH7qHe8WM)]​
    
- the partnership was invaluable  
    invaluable = “极其宝贵”（比 very valuable 还强）。[[youtube](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=nrjH7qHe8WM)]​

## 9. 常见口语反馈与夸奖

- looking good out there, keep pushing  
    场上无线电常用：看起来状态不错，继续加油。keep pushing = 继续发力。[[youtube](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=nrjH7qHe8WM)]​
    
- great job / nice sweat / what a rush  
    great job = 干得漂亮；what a rush = “太刺激了，肾上腺素飙升”。[[youtube](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=nrjH7qHe8WM)]​
    
- it's unforgettable  
    “难以忘怀”。[[youtube](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=nrjH7qHe8WM)]​
